Shot Plan

Photography Journey Planner

Shot Plan is a photography travel planning assistant designed for landscape photographers.

Features

  • Plan photography journeys
  • Save and organize photo locations
  • Create daily shooting schedules
  • View photography routes and maps
  • Prepare drone safety information
